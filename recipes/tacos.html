Quick-and-Easy Fish Tacos

Description

These delightful frozen fish stick tacos can be served with a tossed salad and french fries, or just throw it all together for a quick-and-tasty snack.

Ingredients

  • 30 breaded frozen fish sticks
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 10 corn tortillas
  • ½ pound finely shredded red cabbage
  • ¾ cup tartar sauce
  • ¾ cup salsa

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)
  2. Arrange fish sticks in a single layer on a baking sheet, and bake 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until crisp and golden brown. Remove from heat and cut into thirds.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Fry tortillas until soft. Drain on paper towels.
  4. Fill heated tortillas with fish stick portions, cabbage, tartar sauce, and salsa to serve.
